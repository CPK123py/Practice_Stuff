Community News

Local Parks Renovation Project

The city council has approved a major renovation project for all local parks. The project includes new playground equipment, improved walking paths, and better lighting. Construction will begin next month and is expected to be completed by the end of the year. Community volunteers are welcome to participate in the beautification efforts.

Residents can contribute by joining the Parks Committee or participating in organized volunteer days. The council will provide tools and refreshments for all volunteers. This is a great opportunity to make a positive impact on our community's green spaces.

Event Highlights

Summer Events Calendar

June brings a variety of exciting events to our community. The Summer Festival will feature local musicians, artists, and food vendors. Families are encouraged to bring blankets and chairs for outdoor seating. The festival runs from 6 PM to 10 PM on weekends.

July events include the Community Picnic at Central Park, outdoor movie nights, and fitness classes. August will conclude the summer season with a concert series and neighborhood block parties. All events are free and open to the public.

For more information about any of these events, visit the community center or call the parks department. You can also check our website for regular updates and program schedules. We look forward to seeing you at our upcoming events!
